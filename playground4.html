How to use this tool?

Just enter the fields you'd like to customize. The only mandatory ones are width and height. For the rest of them, the default value is shown in a placeholder.

Tip: If you want the cube to end up in a solved state, just enter "qes" in the preseq field. (seq is the sequence to do when you click the play button, preseq is the moves to perform to a solved cube before showing it, "qes" is just the inverse of "seq" ;))

If you just want to see some random moves, enter "scramble" as seq and "qes" as preseq

When you're done, just copy the iframe code into your own website.
